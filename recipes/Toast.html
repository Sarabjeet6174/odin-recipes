Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 slices of bread (whole wheat, sourdough, or any preferred type)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Lemon juice (optional)
  • Cherry tomatoes or poached egg (optional, for topping)

Steps

  1. Toast the bread: Toast the bread slices until golden brown, either in a toaster or on a skillet.
  2. Prepare the avocado: Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl. Mash with a fork until smooth or leave it slightly chunky, depending on preference.
  3. Season: Add a squeeze of lemon juice, salt, and black pepper to the mashed avocado. Optionally, drizzle with olive oil.
  4. Assemble: Spread the mashed avocado generously on each slice of toasted bread.
  5. Optional toppings: Add red pepper flakes for heat or top with sliced cherry tomatoes or a poached egg for extra flavor.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately as a quick breakfast or snack.
